About DeeRezz

DeeRezz is a multi-talented artist whose musical journey began in 2011 when they delved into creating music using hardware. Transitioning to software production in 2016, DeeRezz marked their presence by releasing tracks on SoundCloud. A brief hiatus saw them exploring the realm of DJ-ing in 2022, reigniting their passion for production in 2023. DeeRezz's debut album, "Ethereal," unveiled in 2023, beautifully captures their evolution and artistry in the realm of electronic music. With a narrative that weaves through hardware experimentation, software innovation, and a newfound vigor for music production, DeeRezz's musical odyssey continues to captivate audiences with its unique blend of sounds and creativity.
